The Incident:
The spotlight has burned out. Tonight, renowned idol Elson Ting collapsed and was found dead backstage immediately following a highly anticipated performance. The stage was set, the audience was blind, and the killer walked away completely unnoticed. We have a body, we have a complex web of suspects, but we do not have the truth.
The Suspects:
Our preliminary sweep indicates several individuals had direct access to the victim, his items, or harbored potential motives:
  • Kayser (Company Director): The powerful executive pulling the strings of Elson's career.
  • CC (The Manager): The handler who oversaw Elson's backstage needs, schedules, and drinks.
  • Ryan (The Assistant): The stressed staff member physically closest to the idol throughout the day.
  • Weihao (Financial Auditor): The company's internal auditor, keeping a close eye on the books.
  • Neo (Fellow Idol): Elson's stage contemporary and suspected rival.
  • Jiongxi (Stage Crew Leader): The technical chief responsible for backstage props and placements.
Your Mission:
Do not look for a monster in the shadows; the true monster awaits in the light. Navigate the game stations, earn tokens, and decrypt the evidence. Beware of false leads and hidden alliances. To win, submit a final report answering:

1. The Mastermind: Who truly orchestrated the murder?
2. The Method: How exactly was the murder carried out mechanically?
3. The Motive: Why did Elson Ting have to die?

The clock is ticking. You have until 9:30 PM. May the performance begin.
